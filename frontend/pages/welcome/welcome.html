Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Overview

BlinkBuddy is a desktop application that monitors eye health metrics using your device's webcam. All data stays local and private.

2. Data We Collect

Webcam frames (processed in real-time, never stored or transmitted), eye health metrics (blink rate, PERCLOS, screen time, head tilt), calibration data, and user settings — all stored locally on your device.

3. Webcam Access

Webcam access is used solely for real-time facial landmark detection. No images or video are saved.

4. Biometric Data

All facial geometry data is processed in memory only and never transmitted externally.

5. Third-Party Services

BlinkBuddy does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data brokers.

6. Contact

For questions, contact the BlinkBuddy development team.
